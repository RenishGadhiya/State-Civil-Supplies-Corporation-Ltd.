State Civil Supplies Corporation Ltd.

About Us

We are State Civil Supplies Corporation Ltd. known as SCSCL.

SCSCL is a government organization that deals with the purchasing and selling of different agricultural commodities. This web application helps the govt. in keeping track of farmers details. The operator (govt employee) can register a farmer with their information. This web application allows to keep track of purchase and distribution of different agricultural commodities.
